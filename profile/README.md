# SmartRetry can turn your declined transactions into revenue!

Every year, billions of legitimate card transactions are declined - and most of that revenue is recoverable. SmartRetry is the **payment recovery platform** that wins it back for merchants and PSPs. We decode issuer signals in real time, intelligently retry **declined card transactions** - soft declines, insufficient-funds, "do not honor", and more - and plug into your existing PSP without a single change to your checkout.

**Resources:** [Docs](https://www.smartretry.com/docs) · [FAQ](https://www.smartretry.com/faq)

**Works with:** Stripe · Adyen · Checkout.com · Worldpay · Braintree · PayPal · Cybersource · and any PSP processing Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and 3DS2 transactions. Plays nicely alongside fraud tools like Stripe Radar.

## How SmartRetry recovers declined transactions

Three real-time steps to turn every decline into a second chance to get paid - all happening in milliseconds, before the customer is gone:

1. **The customer is gone** - a real buyer with valid payment gets declined by a rigid payment system.
2. **We find what really went wrong** - real-time diagnostics uncover the cause (3DS, browser errors, card blocks, geo mismatches) and rebuild the transaction smarter on the fly.
3. **You get paid** - the retry runs in real time, the customer pays, the revenue comes back. You only pay us when it works.

## Payment recovery capabilities

Three plays from the **SmartRetry Arsenal** - just a glimpse of the hundreds of tactics working behind the scenes for you.

### Familiar transaction recognition
We surface a past payment this customer completed successfully with similar characteristics, and frame the new attempt in that context. The issuer sees a transaction they've already approved - and say yes again.

<img width="700" height="393.75" alt="SmartRetry Arsenal - issuer context recognition using past successful transactions" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bd2a8960-484c-492f-821f-c445f0d3cfab" />

### Smart routing
We pick the ideal acquirer or payment path for each transaction and send it down the lane most likely to succeed. Same payment, smarter road.

<img width="700" height="393.75" alt="SmartRetry Arsenal - smart routing through the optimal acquirer or PSP path" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e020b3b8-3e5d-4ebf-98fa-dcffc7d327b5" />

### One-parameter recovery
Sometimes a single field is the only thing wrong. Instead of failing the entire transaction (and losing the customer to friction), we surgically prompt them to fix just that detail - the rest of the flow stays alive.
